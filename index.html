Intro

Hello, my name is Levi. I'm a full stack software developer with experience as a web technical lead, and product delivery lead at Lambda School. I've had the opportunity to work with and lead teams consisting of Web developers, Data scientists, UX designers, and Android developers.

Our teams have collaborated to build and deploy full stack web and mobile applications from the ground up, with varying integrations of data science modeling, UX wireframing, and mobile first development.

About

I started my education as a pre-pharmacy major at Oregon State University. I was there for 2 years, before I realized I had not yet found a career route I was passionate in.

Afterwards, I worked for over four years in the Manufacturing industry in various quality and production roles for companies like Intel and Maxim Integrated. My work in the Manufacturing Tech industry allowed me to gain valuble experience collaborating with talented teams to ship products based on client needs and industry standard procedures. During my training at Intel, I was introduced to the software and web development field.

In November 2018, I enrolled in Lambda School's full stack web development curriculum, it was there that I discovered my passion is technology and creating impactful web applications that can improve lives.

I currently live in the beautiful city of Portland Oregon with my husband. We enjoy kayaking in the scapoose river, and exploring Oregon's unique trails and rivers.
